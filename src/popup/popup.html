LISA Core - AI Memory Library

What is LISA Core?

LISA translates your AI conversations into structured, portable data — so you never lose context, never start over, and never get locked into one platform.

Capture from 9 AI platforms. Continue anywhere. Your data stays yours.

Export & Capture

Extension popup: Click "Export Conversation" to extract and compress.

Floating button: On any AI chat, click LISA then choose LISA Raw (archiving) or LISA-V (AI handoffs with SHA-256 integrity).

Selection: Right-click selected text then "Export to LISA".

Phoenix Rebirth

The pressure gauge tracks your context usage in real time. When it runs low:

  1. Click Rebirth on the gauge
  2. Choose a mode: Distilled, Full, or Semantic
  3. LISA opens a fresh session with your full context carried over

Works same-platform or cross-platform — start on ChatGPT, continue on Claude.

Library

Saves tab: Your exported conversations (LISA-V, Raw, Compressed).

Rebirths tab: Your Phoenix rebirth handoffs.

Click the inject button on any entry to inject it into your current AI chat as a .md handoff.

Continue Anywhere

Upload any LISA file to a new AI and say:

"Read this and continue our conversation"

The AI picks up exactly where you left off — decisions, code, open tasks, all of it.
